Breaking Bad

A high school chemistry teacher diagnosed with inoperable lung cancer turns to manufacturing and selling methamphetamine in order to secure his family's future. What starts as a desperate plan transforms Walter White from a mild-mannered teacher into a ruthless player in the drug trade.

Main Characters

Walter White

A brilliant but underachieving chemistry teacher who transforms into a ruthless drug kingpin under the alias "Heisenberg".

Jesse Pinkman

A former student of Walter White who becomes his partner in the meth business, bringing street knowledge to their operation.

Saul Goodman

A criminal lawyer who helps Walter and Jesse navigate the legal and illegal aspects of their business with creative solutions.

Gustavo Fring

A sophisticated drug kingpin who hides in plain sight as a successful restaurant chain owner.

Best Episodes For Me

S1E1: Pilot

S2E9: 4 Days Out

S5E16: Felina

The series finale where Walt returns to Albuquerque to settle his scores and tie up loose ends.
